About Us

Swiggy is an Indian online food ordering and delivery platform. Founded in 2014, Swiggy is headquartered in Bangalore and operates in more than 500 Indian cities as of September 2021.[5][6] Besides food delivery, the platform also provides on-demand grocery deliveries under the name Instamart, and same-day package delivery service called Swiggy Genie.

  • In 2019, the company acqui-hired the artificial intelligence startup Kint.io.
  • In 2017, Swiggy acqui-hired Bangalore-based Asian food start-up 48East.
  • In 2019, Swiggy, invested ₹31 crore in Mumbai-based ready-to-eat food brand Fingerlix
  • In 2022, Swiggy led a $180 million Series D investment round in bike taxi company Rapido.

    How much does Swiggy pay per delivery?

    The average Swiggy delivery boy makes between Rs. 15-20 per order. However, this can vary depending on the location, time of day, and type of order.

    How is Swiggy salary calculated?

    A Swiggy spokesperson said a delivery worker's earnings comprise three main components – per order payout, which is proportional to factors like distance traveled and time taken, surge pay, and incentive pay. Swiggy also said their delivery workers earned over 20 percent more in July 2021 than they did in January 2020.
